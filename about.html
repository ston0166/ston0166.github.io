Meet the creator...

Hey! My name is Melanie, I am 18 years old, and I am from Ottawa, Ontario Canada. I specialize in Videography, but dabble in photography and web development as well.

Some of my current experience consists of in school video production work, creating stop motion video and working with Adobe Premiere and After Effects to create seamless films.

For long term in my career, I am going to attend the Toronto Film School to further my current knowledge of film studies and further my career as an actress as well!

Some general information about me, can be that I love horror and all things creepy ( as well as comedy), science fiction, spicy food, and especially superheroes (Deadpool and Spider-Man are my go-to's).
